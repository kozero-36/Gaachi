Gachi 3C Analysis

Customer
(Two-Sided Market)
Authors (Supply Side)
  • Demographics: Ages 50-80, retired professionals
  • Pain Points: Retirement blues, social isolation, loss of identity
  • Needs: Self-affirmation, legacy building, supplemental income
  • Market Size: 45M people aged 60+ in Japan/Korea
Readers (Demand Side)
  • Demographics: Ages 25-45, career professionals
  • Pain Points: Job transfers, career changes, specific life challenges
  • Needs: Ultra-specific experiential knowledge (e.g., "Fukuoka manufacturing transfer 1988")
  • Willingness to Pay: ¥2,000-5,000 per book
Competitor
(Market Landscape)
Direct Competitors (Memoir)
  • StoryWorth/Remento: Family-pays model, text or basic AI, no marketplace
  • Tell Mel: Similar AI tech, English only, premium pricing
  • 自分史.com: ¥220K author-pays, manual editing, no scalability
Adjacent Competitors (Knowledge)
  • note: Creative writing platform, young users, no elderly focus
  • Brain: Business know-how, exaggeration risk, different demographic
  • Udemy: Systematic courses, not personal experiential stories
Major Threat
  • MyHeritage Stories: Large user base + recent AI voice feature = potential 2027 entry
